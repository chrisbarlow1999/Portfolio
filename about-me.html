About Me

I am a Project Manager with experience delivering large-scale EPOS deployments across stadiums, festivals, and entertainment venues throughout the UK. During my time at Kappture I led projects ranging from small installations to full stadium rollouts involving over 1,000 payment terminals.

I enjoy working on projects that involve real operational challenges — particularly environments where technology must be implemented quickly and reliably, such as live events, sports venues, and festivals. My experience includes coordinating engineering teams, managing stakeholders, producing project documentation, and ensuring technology deployments are ready for live operational environments.
