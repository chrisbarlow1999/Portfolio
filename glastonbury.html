Glastonbury Festival

Client

Glastonbury Festival

My Role

Project Manager

Project Scope

Short term EPOS Deployment (Rental)

Project Overview

This project involved the deployment of over 600 mobile handheld terminals on a rental basis during peak festival season. All terminals had to be set up, configured, and tested under extremely tight deadlines to ensure smooth festival operations.

Due to limited internet access on-site, a new offline transaction processing workflow was implemented to ensure uninterrupted sales. Over 12 staff members worked in shifts to provide on-site support and training to festival personnel within a very short timeframe.

Project Challenges

The project was highly time-sensitive, with strict deadlines to meet before the festival opened. Key challenges included configuring and deploying hardware quickly, training festival staff on new systems, and ensuring all transactions could be processed reliably offline. Coordination with multiple stakeholders was critical to meet the festival’s operational requirements.

My Responsibilities

  • Managed the deployment of 600+ handheld terminals across the festival site
  • Coordinated on-site staff shifts and provided training for festival personnel
  • Developed and implemented offline transaction processing to cope with limited connectivity
  • Ensured hardware and software configurations were completed within tight deadlines
  • Oversaw daily operational support and troubleshooting throughout the festival
  • Produced documentation and guides for festival staff on terminal usage and troubleshooting

Implementation

Terminals were pre-configured prior to the festival and then deployed rapidly across multiple festival locations. A dedicated team of 12 staff members worked in shifts to support setup, train festival personnel, and troubleshoot any issues during the event.

An offline transaction system was implemented to ensure sales continuity, and reporting processes were adapted to sync with central systems once connectivity was available. This ensured accurate tracking of sales and product data throughout the festival.

Results

  • Successfully deployed 600+ mobile terminals across Glastonbury Festival within a short timeframe
  • Implemented reliable offline transaction processing under limited connectivity conditions
  • Provided training and on-site support to festival staff, ensuring smooth operations
  • Delivered a fully functional system for the duration of the festival, meeting all operational requirements
