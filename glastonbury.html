Project Overview

This project involved the deployment of over 600 mobile handheld terminals on a rental basis during peak festival season. All terminals had to be set up, configured, and tested under extremely tight deadlines.

Due to limited internet access on-site, a new offline transaction processing workflow was implemented to ensure uninterrupted sales. Over 12 staff members worked in shifts to provide on-site support and training within a very short timeframe.

My Responsibilities

  • Managed the deployment of 600+ handheld terminals across the festival site
  • Coordinated on-site staff shifts and provided training for festival personnel
  • Developed and implemented offline transaction processing to cope with limited connectivity
  • Ensured hardware and software configurations were completed within tight deadlines
  • Oversaw daily operational support and troubleshooting throughout the festival
  • Produced documentation and guides for festival staff on terminal usage

Implementation

Terminals were pre-configured prior to the festival and deployed rapidly across multiple locations. A dedicated team of 12 staff worked in shifts to support setup, train personnel, and troubleshoot issues.

An offline transaction system was implemented to ensure sales continuity, with reporting processes adapted to sync with central systems once connectivity was restored.

Results

✓  600+ mobile terminals deployed across Glastonbury within a short timeframe
✓  Offline transaction processing implemented reliably under limited connectivity
✓  Training and on-site support delivered to festival staff ensuring smooth operations
✓  Fully functional system maintained for the entire duration of the festival
