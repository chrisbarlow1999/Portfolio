Project Overview

This project involved replacing the existing EPOS infrastructure across Everton Football Club's stadium with Kappture's payment platform, including approximately 500 payment terminals across multiple concession areas.

The scale of the deployment required careful coordination to ensure all terminals were installed, configured, and ready for use before matchday operations resumed.

My Responsibilities

  • Managed the full rollout of approximately 500 EPOS terminals across the stadium
  • Managed all project documentation including RAID logs, project plans and progress tracking
  • Coordinated installation timelines with stadium operations and catering teams
  • Oversaw terminal configuration and system testing before go-live
  • Ensured staff were prepared to transition from the previous EPOS provider
  • Monitored system performance during initial matchday operations

Implementation

The project focused on a complete EPOS swap-out from the previous supplier to Kappture's platform. Terminals were deployed across all operational areas including bars, kiosks, and hospitality locations.

Careful planning ensured the installation was completed efficiently with minimal disruption to stadium operations. All terminals were configured, tested, and validated ahead of the first matchday.

Results

✓  Approximately 500 EPOS terminals successfully replaced across the stadium
✓  Project delivered on schedule ahead of matchday operations
✓  Smooth transition from the previous EPOS supplier achieved
✓  Stable and reliable payment platform established for stadium concessions
