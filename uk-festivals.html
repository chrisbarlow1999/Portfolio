Project Overview

This project involved implementing mobile payment terminals across a variety of UK festivals for the first time. These festivals varied in size, layout, products, staff, and each required a unique approach to deployment. I developed the processes to deliver and support Kappture’s terminals efficiently under very tight deadlines.

Festivals Implemented

  • Radio 1 Road Show
  • 2000 Tree's
  • Arc Tangent
  • Local Charity Festival
  • Clockstock
  • Matts BBQ
  • Sign of the Times
  • Stone Valley South
  • Wannasee South

My Responsibilities

  • Developed and documented processes for terminal implementation across multiple festivals
  • Coordinated deliveries and setup schedules under strict time constraints
  • Trained festival staff on terminal usage and pre-order processes
  • Managed support staff teams to provide on-site assistance during festivals
  • Created standardized procedures to handle differences in layouts, products, and banking requirements
  • Ensured a smooth and repeatable process for future festival deployments

Implementation

Each festival presented unique challenges. Different team sizes, product lines, and payment methods required flexible yet repeatable procedures. I implemented a fast-track process for delivering, configuring, and supporting the mobile terminals, ensuring minimal disruption to festival operations.

Training materials were adapted for different staff groups, and on-site support was coordinated across shifts. This project established the first operational framework for Kappture’s mobile terminal rentals at festivals.

Results

  • Successfully implemented terminals at multiple UK festivals under tight timescales
  • Created a scalable process for future festival deployments
  • Enabled festival staff to use terminals efficiently, improving customer experience
  • Established Kappture’s first festival rental program with proven procedures
